Pasta Recipe

Description

This pasta recipe is quick and easy to make, perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion. It features a rich tomato sauce and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce to the pan and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Drain the pasta and add it to the sauce, mixing well.
  5. Serve with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese on top.
